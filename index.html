CSGoBomb Game Instructions

  • Goal is to mark all Terrorist Bomb by RIGHT clicking on the box
  • Opening box areas that contain no bomb can be looked at by a LEFT click on your mouse
  • Left clicking your mouse will give you a number which can be a hint on your next move
  • You lose and die in the game if you click on a planted bomb
  • You won the game if you open all the hidden boxes and mark all the bombs
